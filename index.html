More About Me

During my PhD research work at German Aerospace Center (DLR-Berlin), I got to work with 3D reconstruction, SLAM and created a generic framework (named SmoothFusion) for working with RGBD camera, setereo cameras, and LiDAR based data.
The framework is created solely by me in C++ using OpenCV, OpenVDB, and many other libraries.

After PhD, had a pleasure of working with traditional lossless image processing and compression systems such as Jpeg-LS, Jpeg-2k and implemented a preprocessor module to increase the compression ratios in this Patent

Keeping up with currnet trends of market (i.e. AI and ML research), switched to python for fast prototyping and developed many projects (see below for reference).
